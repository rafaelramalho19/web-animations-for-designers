### Lessons to be learned

<ul>
    <li class="fragment fade-up fade-in-then-semi-out">Don't underestimate simple animations.</li>
    <li class="fragment fade-down fade-in-then-semi-out">Think of edge-cases.</li>
    <li class="fragment fade-in-then-semi-out">Try not to make the animation the <strong>core</strong> of your feature.</li>
</ul>

---

### How to improve as a Product Owner?

<ul>
    <li class="fragment fade-down fade-in-then-semi-out">Raise awareness about animation necessity to stakeholders.</li>
    <li class="fragment fade-in-then-semi-out">Hear the developers and designers.</li>
    <li class="fragment fade-in-then-semi-out">Look into the product requirements and find places to animate.</li>
</ul>
